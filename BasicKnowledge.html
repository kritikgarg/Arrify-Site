What is CRM : Use, Benefits and Future in the next 10 Years

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It refers to a business strategy that involves managing interactions and relationships with customers and potential customers. The goal of CRM is to improve customer satisfaction, loyalty, and retention by providing personalized experiences and delivering relevant information and offers. The use of CRM has become increasingly widespread in recent years, as businesses of all sizes seek to improve their customer engagement and retention. By leveraging the data and insights provided by CRM systems, businesses can better understand their customers' needs and preferences, and tailor their interactions accordingly.
