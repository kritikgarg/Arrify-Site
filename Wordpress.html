How to connect gravity forms to Salesforce

Gravity Forms is a popular WordPress plugin that enables users to create a range of customizable forms, including contact forms, surveys, and quizzes. On the other hand, Salesforce is a powerful cloud-based CRM platform that helps businesses manage customer data, sales processes, and marketing campaigns. To connect gravity forms to Salesforce, users can capture data submitted through forms and automatically sync it with Salesforce, eliminating the need for manual data entry. For example, if you have a contact form on your website, you can map the form fields to the corresponding contact fields in Salesforce, such as first name, last name, email address, phone number, etc. When a visitor submits the form, their contact information will be added to your Salesforce database automatically.
