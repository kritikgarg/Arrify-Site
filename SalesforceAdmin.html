Objects in Salesforce

In Salesforce, Objects are an entity that used by user mainly to store customers data. Their structures are exactly same as any excel spreadsheet has, such as rows and columns. They stores customers data in a table form which table represent as an object, columns indicate as object fields and row indicate as a record of object.

Opportunity in Salesforce

Opportunity objects are used to store information about the deal which happen between customers or organisation. For example, Company Arrify deal the 50 products of PC with a manufacture company. All the information related to that deal going to store into opportunity object.
